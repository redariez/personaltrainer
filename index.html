MY JOURNEY

“Never judge a book by its cover” You never know what kind journey each of us have been on. When people see me most people automatically think that I’m fit, I’m healthy, in shape, and I don’t know what it is like to be over weight or down someone else’s path. One thing that most people don’t know about me is that I’m so far from anything perfect. I struggle every day, I don’t eat healthy, I get lazy, and at times and I don’t even go to the gym. In 2015 I was over 210 lbs and in May of this year I got really lazy and gained over 30 lbs. When I say I’ve been down your path, trust me when I say it. I may not have been down your exact path, but I’ve been down mine and mine has been tough, hard, far from easy, and sometimes not even worth it. I sometimes on the daily need to find my inner strength just to get through. One thing I new was in the end it some how would all be worth it. Whether it’s about fitness, life struggles, or just a tough time remember we are all stronger than we think. As long as your dedicated, motivated, determined, and have self discipline I promise it will all be worth it. Remember in everything we do, we don’t gain without pain. You’ll never know what victory feels like without feeling the pain of defeat. This isn’t just your journey it’s ours. So let’s do this and get you to where you want to be.
