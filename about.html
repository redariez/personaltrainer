A LITTLE ABOUT MY JOURNEY

My name is Tessie and back in 2015 I weighed in at 220 lbs and wore a size 18. At that time I thought I was happy, healthy, and in shape. Doctors would always mention my heart problem and recommended me to lose at least 50 lbs. Although I was highly aware of my Heart Issue I still didn’t listen and I let myself continue to be only 5’4 weighing 70 lbs over my recommended height to weight ratio I was considered overly obese. In early 2015 my best friend passed away due to an over dose. It was a hard time in my life accepting such a drastic life change that I started to self destruct. I went down such a rough patch that the only person that could have my back was God. I hit rock bottom and at that exact moment I new I had to make some major life changes, including losing the weight my doctors requested of me. I new if I wanted to live longer than my best friend, if I wanted to be healthy, if I truly wanted to be happy it was Time to take on the journey of a lifetime.

Yes I contemplated weight loss surgery, diet pills, waist trainers, and so forth. However; I decided to take the best route for me, and that was to lose the weight as natural as possible.
